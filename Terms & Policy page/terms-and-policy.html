What is WhatTheFood?

WhatTheFood is a website dedicated to finding new recipes, uploading your own recipes and learning how to cook.


What can you find here?

Here you can:

  • make an account and upload your own recipes.
  • upload photos of the recipes you have tried from this website.
  • search recipes based on its name or on certain ingredients.
  • order the search results by popularity, duration, difficulty, most recently added and alphabetically.
  • see some rankings such as the most popular recipes and ingredients and the most unliked ingredients.

WhatTheFood is free

WhatTheFood is and will always be a completely free source of recipes.
